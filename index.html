Features

Built for the way your mind actually works.

One hotkey. Zero friction.

Hit Ctrl+Shift+Space from anywhere — a code editor, a browser, a PDF. FocusTap appears. Type your task. Hit Enter. It's gone. Back to what you were doing, no context switch, no app to close.

Natural language input parses priority and tags inline. Type Finish Q3 report !h #work and FocusTap sets priority to High and tags it #work automatically.

Designed with ADHD in brain.

Every interaction is optimized for the way distractible brains actually work — not how productivity books say they should. No punishment mechanics. No streak guilt. No overwhelming dashboards. Just a calm, dark surface that waits patiently until you need it.

Pomodoro without the tab.

Start a focus session on any task with Ctrl+Shift+P. A clean progress ring counts down. When the session ends, a gentle chime — not a blaring alarm — signals the break. Work and break durations are customizable.

Understand when you actually work.

FocusTap tracks when you complete tasks and run focus sessions, then surfaces the patterns: your most productive hour, your best day of the week, your consistency score. A GitHub-style streak calendar shows your progress at a glance — without turning into a guilt trip on off days.

Your tasks, on your calendar.

Pro

Connect Google Calendar or Microsoft 365. FocusTap pulls events into your day view and pushes tasks with due dates to your calendar — two-way sync, no manual duplication. OAuth tokens stored in your OS keychain, never in a database.

Five presets. Zero Comic Sans.

Midnight (the default OLED-dark glassmorphism), Aurora (deep blue with teal accents), Sepia (warm paper tones for eye comfort), Evergreen (forest green calm), and Monochrome (pure grayscale minimalism). Each works in dark and light modes.
